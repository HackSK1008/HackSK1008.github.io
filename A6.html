Aegis Graham Bell Award by Government of India

Honoured with the National Aegis Graham Bell Award for Innovation in AI & Data Science, presented by Shri Jayant Chaudhary, Minister of State for Electronics & IT. Recognized for the Saheli project — India’s first AI-powered women’s safety platform — selected from 1,200+ national nominations for social impact and technical originality.

Highlights
  • Presenter: Shri Jayant Chaudhary, MoS Electronics & IT
  • Project: Saheli — AI-based women’s safety platform
  • Selection: Top national innovations (1,200+ nominations)
Impact

The award showcases the social and technical importance of Saheli and underlines readiness for policy-level adoption and large-scale deployments for public safety.
